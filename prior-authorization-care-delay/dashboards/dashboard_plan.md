# Dashboard Plan — Prior Authorization & Care Delay

## Primary Decision
Which encounter types should Claims Operations prioritize to reduce high-cost claim delays?

## Intended Audience
Claims Operations leadership and process improvement teams.

## Core Views
1. Delay bucket distribution (claim volume by delay range)
2. Average claim cost by delay bucket
3. Average delay by encounter type
4. Encounter types with both high delay and high cost

## Design Notes
- Focus on ranking and comparison, not trends
- Highlight delays exceeding 30 days
- Use clear labels and minimal color
